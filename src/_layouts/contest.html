Join the Vision Developer contest!

Build an awesome app with the Mapbox Vision SDK, and enter for a chance to win an all-expense-paid trip to a end AWS Re:Invent 2018. Plus, receive free AWS credits to help you begin building!

We’re looking for the most innovative app to help drivers, consumers, or businesses explore their world and connect with people around them. Using the Mapbox Vision SDK, we want to see how you utilize real-time navigation, computer vision, augmented reality and live mapping capabilities to build an app that creates new value for people on the road.

Register for the Vision Developer Contest

We’re excited to partner with AWS on this contest. The first 250 eligible contest participants to register will receive $100 worth of free AWS credits to help you get started, and one developer will win an all-expense-paid trip to attend AWS Re:Invent this November (11/25/18 - 11/30/18).

Here are some ideas to help you get started:

  • Create an augmented reality navigation experience that helps on-demand drivers easily find their pickup and drop-off targets.
  • Build a “smart city” map that helps local governments better monitor and manage road and traffic conditions to improve the flow in cities.
  • Create an AR videogame for passengers that brings characters to life right on the landscape outside the car window.

Contest Details

Full contest rules here

  1. Register for the contest above. You’ll receive instructions via email to download Mapbox’s Vision SDK and to access your free AWS storage credits.
  2. Build an app that offers a unique take on computer vision, location-based augmented reality or navigation. We’ll be looking for the most innovative app the integrates Vision SDK most successfully to provide new value to people in the car and on the road.
  3. Submit your app to Testflight or Google Play and email the link to vision@mapbox.com no later than November 16th, 2018 by 8pm PT. Include a description in your email that answers the following questions:
    • What does your app do? What value does it provide?
    • Who is the primary audience for your app and why will they be excited about it?
    • How have you incorporated Vision SDK into the app?

One winning developer will receive an all-expense-paid trip to attend AWS Re:Invent from November 25th, 2018 - November 30th, 2018, including round-trip airfare, 5 nights at a hotel in Las Vegas and a free ticket to attend the conference. The winner will be notified on November 19th and will have to accept their prize no later than November 21st, 2018.

Submission Requirements:

  • The app must integrate Mapbox’s Vision SDK.
  • The app must be submi ed via Testflight or Google Play for the judges to evaluate it.
  • Contest is open for U.S. Residents only.
