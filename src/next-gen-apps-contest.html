Contest Details

See full contest rules here

  1. Register for the contest above. You’ll receive instructions via email to download Mapbox’s Vision SDK and to access your free AWS storage credits.
  2. Build an app that offers a unique take on computer vision, location-based augmented reality or navigation. We’ll be looking for the most innovative app the integrates Vision SDK most successfully to provide new value to people in the car and on the road.
  3. Submit your app to Testflight and email the link to vision@mapbox.com no later than November 16th, 2018 by 8pm PT. Include a description in your email that answers the following questions:
    • What does your app do? What value does it provide?
    • Who is the primary audience for your app? Why would they care?
    • How have you incorporated Vision SDK into the app?

One winning developer will receive an all-expense-paid trip to a end AWS Re:Invent from November 25th, 2018 - November 30th, 2018, including round-trip airfare, 5 nights at a hotel in Las Vegas and a free ticket to a end the conference. Winners will be notified on November 19th and will have to accept their prize no later than November 21st, 2018.

Submission Requirements:

  • The app must integrate Mapbox’s Vision SDK.
  • The app must be submitted via Testflight for the judges to evaluate it.
  • Contest is open for U.S. Residents only.
